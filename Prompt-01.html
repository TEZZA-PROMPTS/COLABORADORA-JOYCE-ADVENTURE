VISUAL STYLE
Realistic 3D avatar style inspired by IMVU and Second Life, enhanced realism, smooth mesh topology, detailed skin shader, glossy realistic hair cards, stylized yet semi-realistic facial proportions, fashion-focused aesthetic.

SCENE & ENVIRONMENT
Car interior environment with a modern luxury feel, black leather seats and panoramic roof clearly visible above. Side window reveals an outdoor urban structure (bridge/metal framework) slightly blurred. Interior is dark, contrasting with bright daylight entering. Clean, reflective surfaces, subtle depth of field.

LIGHTING
Natural daylight entering from the left side of the frame, casting stronger illumination on the man’s face and softer, slightly diffused light on the woman. Highlights visible on sunglasses and glossy jacket. Realistic contrast with natural shadow falloff and minimal artificial fill.

CAMERA & FRAMING
Vertical composition, medium close-up (chest-up framing), 50mm lens, realistic perspective.
Front-facing phone camera positioned slightly to the left side of both subjects and held at approximately eye level (not low angle).
Camera is subtly tilted counterclockwise (left side slightly higher), creating a natural diagonal selfie composition.
Distance is very close, causing slight perspective distortion: the man (left) appears marginally larger and closer, while the woman (right) appears slightly farther and angled inward.
Tight framing with both faces filling the image, minimal depth-of-field blur, realistic reflections on lenses.

Head turned slightly left toward him, with a gentle inward tilt.
Pose: right hand supporting her cheek, fingers softly curved along the jawline and cheek, creating a relaxed resting pose.
Wearing oversized black sunglasses with semi-transparent amber/orange lenses (eyes faintly visible through tint), small gold hoop earrings, and a black patent leather jacket with strong specular highlights and visible folds.

CHARACTER 2 – BLACK MAN
High-poly 3D male avatar with short black hair styled upward with volume and natural texture. Fair skin with realistic tone variation and light stubble.

Head slightly angled toward the woman, positioned closer to the lens due to camera placement.
Wearing black rectangular sunglasses with dark opaque lenses and a black puffer jacket with inflated segments and semi-gloss reflections. Leaning inward, occupying slightly more foreground presence.
