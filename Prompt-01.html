Ultra-realistic cinematic female portrait — Blender Cycles render.

CAMERA & COMPOSITION
close-up framing with a slightly elevated top-down angle, subject lying on a soft surface, centered composition with balanced negative space and a fashion editorial aesthetic, shallow depth of field subtly softening the background.

POSE & BODY POSITIONING
relaxed reclined pose with both arms raised and bent, one arm positioned above the head holding a flower while the other supports the head, shoulders slightly angled creating a natural S-curve in the body.

HEAD POSITION & GAZE
head tilted gently toward the camera with a slight sideways inclination, gaze directed straight into the lens with an intimate and engaging presence 

FACIAL EXPRESSION
calm, soft, and subtly seductive expression with slightly parted lips and relaxed eyelids conveying elegance and confidence.

FACE & IDENTITY
symmetrical and refined facial structure with high cheekbones, sculpted jawline, straight delicate nose, full lips with a glossy finish, and defined brows framing almond-shaped eyes with long voluminous lashes, maintaining a polished and harmonious identity.

HAIR
very light platinum blonde hair on top with a smooth transition into pastel pink ends, styled in soft voluminous waves cascading across the pillow, silky texture with natural shine and controlled flow. 

ACCESSORIES & DETAILS
gold hoop earrings, a delicate gold bracelet, and a metallic wristwatch adding a luxurious touch, the white flower is positioned on the side of the head, placed just above the left ear. It rests gently between the hair and the hand supporting the head, sitting near the temple and blending into the wavy strands. This placement creates a delicate focal point that contrasts softly with the light hair and enhances the overall elegant facial composition.

OUTFIT
sleeveless metallic gold top with a smooth reflective fabric that catches highlights, fitted to the body and emphasizing elegance and minimalism.

BODY & PHYSIQUE
slender and proportionate physique with smooth contours, natural posture and refined feminine silhouette.

SKIN & REALISM
flawless, luminous skin with soft highlights and subtle glow, detailed pores and realistic texture, warm undertones enhanced by diffused light.

LIGHTING
soft, warm, diffused lighting coming from above and slightly to the side, creating gentle shadows and enhancing facial contours while maintaining a glowing skin effect.

ENVIRONMENT
minimalistic soft bedding background in neutral tones, clean and uncluttered setting emphasizing the subject, fabric textures slightly visible adding depth. 

MOOD & AESTHETIC
luxurious, romantic, and editorial fashion mood with a serene and intimate atmosphere.

STYLE & RENDER QUALITY
ultra-detailed 8K resolution, cinematic color grading with warm tones, high dynamic range, hyper-realistic rendering with refined light reflections and professional fashion photography finish.
